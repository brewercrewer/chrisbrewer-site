Best Retreats & The Retreat Safety Project
bestretreats.co · Founded 2021
● 501(c)(3) Nonprofit

Best Retreats is an independent safety intelligence platform for the ayahuasca retreat industry — no bookings, no operator payments, no incentive to look the other way. Built on the same principles as consumer protection and compliance infrastructure: screening standards, incident transparency, and accountability for operators who hold significant power over vulnerable participants.

The Retreat Safety Project, Inc. is the Missouri-incorporated 501(c)(3) nonprofit behind the platform, focused on harm reduction, facilitator accountability, and honest research in a category where polished marketing regularly outpaces basic safety standards.
